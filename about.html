Our Story

Born from Passion, Powered by AI

Viral Scale AI was founded in 2022 by a group of digital natives who saw a gap in the market: brands desperate to go viral but without the tools, time, or expertise to make it happen consistently.

We combined deep expertise in video production, growth marketing, and AI tooling to create a full-service agency that delivers enterprise-level content at startup speed.

In partnership with a leading British Media Company, we operate globally — serving clients across the UK, North America, Europe, and beyond. Our diverse team brings creative excellence and global perspective to every campaign.
