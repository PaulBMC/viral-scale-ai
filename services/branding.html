Our Process

How We Do It

A collaborative four-phase process that takes you from blank canvas to a fully realised brand system.

01

Discovery & Positioning

Brand questionnaire, mood board creation, and a comprehensive mapping of the competitive landscape to find your unique space.

02

Concept Development

Three distinct brand direction concepts, each with logo, colour, and typography previews — presented with rationale for each.

03

Refinement & System Build

Refine the chosen direction through revision rounds, then build the complete brand system with every asset and template.

04

Delivery & Guidelines

Final file delivery in all formats (AI, EPS, PNG, SVG), plus a comprehensive brand guidelines document your whole team can use.
